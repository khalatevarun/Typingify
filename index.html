Rules and tips

1) The timer starts as soon as you start typing in the text area.

2) To stop the current recording, press 'Enter' and the score will be recored in the table.

3) To restart the recording, press 'Escape' and the parameters will be set back to inital values.

4) There are two modes.

a) Easy - Sentences without punctations with all characters in lower case.

b) Difficult - Sentences with punctations and following the basic rules of grammar.

Words = Total Characters Typed / 5

Errors = No. of Incorrect Characters Typed

Accuracy = ( Correct Characters Typed / Total Characters Typed ) * 100 %

CPM = ( Correct Characters Typed / Duration ) * 60

WPM = CPM/5
